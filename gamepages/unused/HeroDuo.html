Hero Duo

Time frame: June 2023

Role: Gameplay Developer, Designer, UI and Audio

Tools: Unity and Visual Studio

Languages: C#

Team:


Description:

Hero Duo is a 2D shoot 'em up made in collaboration with the students from Kyoto Computer Gakuin during a 24 hour game jam. In Hero Duo, you can switch between two playable characters - Taro and Eagle - to defeat the legendary Godfoot, a combination of Godzilla and Bigfoot. The background is filled with scenery from Japan and the United States, such as Mt. Fuji with a piece of sushi on top, and the Statue of Liberty with a hamburger in place of the torch. This game was a fun combination of our cultures.

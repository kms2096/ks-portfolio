Wyrdsong

Time frame: June 2023 to August 2023

Role: Junior Game Developer

Tools: Unreal Engine 5, Perforce, Jira, Visual Studio

Languages: C++

Team: Something Wicked Games


Description:

Wyrdsong is the new AAA open world preternatural RPG from Something Wicked Games, a new, globally dispersed AAA games studio led by Jeff Gardiner, a 15-year veteran of Bethesda (Skyrim, Fallout 3/4/76) and co-founder, Charles Staples (The Outer Worlds, Fallout NV), with creative and technical lead talent from Obsidian Entertainment, Bioware, and Bethesda. For more information on Something Wicked Games and updates to Wyrdsong, please visit www.somethingwickedgames.com.


Development:

During Summer 2023 I was a Junior Game Developer that worked on Wyrdsong using a combination of Perforce, Unreal Engine 5, Jira, Blueprints, and Visual studio to create UMG widgets and new gameplay features. I also created Technical Design Documents for anything that I implemented, documenting how to use them. In addition to my assigned tasks, I also play tested daily builds, and thoroughly documented any bugs or glitches.

All work is under NDA and cannot be shown until game release.
